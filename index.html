Frequently Asked Questions

We require all appointments to be cancelled or rescheduled within in 48 hours or more of their appointment time & day. Appointments that are not cancelled prior to the 48 hour window are subject to a cancellation fee of half the service. There is a 15 minute grace period for all late appointments. After the grace period appointments will be considered a no show and may be charged for their missed appointment, or may not have time to be styled or complete the full service, upon the stylistâ€™s discretion. Appointments that are no call/no show are subject to a fee of the full service price.

A $30 nonrefundable deposit is required for all color services for new clients to hold their spot. A deposit of up to 50% of the service is required for extension and color correction services. The amount of the deposit will go towards the total of the service. If the appointment is rescheduled within 48 hours notice your deposit will rollover to the new appointment date/time. A credit card is required to schedule/book an appointment, this is put in place to ensure the cancellation policy.
